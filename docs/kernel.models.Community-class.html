                             Community

Community model

Community is the secondary organization structure. Residing under cities, they can be neighborhoods, business districts, college campuses, and so on. Each city has a pseudo community that represents the city itself.

For example:

Saint Louis (City)

  • Saint Louis (Community)
  • Central West End (Community)
  • Washington University in St. Louis (Community)
  • The Loop Business District (Community)
